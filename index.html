Aim of the game

Be the first player to reach a 100 global score.

  • This game has two players playing in rounds.
  • On each turn a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish. Each roll adds to their round score
  • If the player rolls a 1, they lost their round score. It is then the other players turn.
  • The player can choose to hold. Which means that their round score is added to their global score.
